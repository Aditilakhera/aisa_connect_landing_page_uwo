# PART 2: PRIVACY POLICY

**Last Updated:** May 18, 2026

At **AISA Connect™**, we are deeply committed to protecting the privacy and security of your business data, your customer information, and your personal details. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, process, store, and safeguard data when you use AISA Connect™.

---

### 1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
* **Account Information:** Name, business email address, phone number, physical address, company details, and billing/payment credentials.
* **Integration Data:** API keys, access tokens, WhatsApp Business Account (WABA) IDs, and configuration parameters required to connect with your Meta Developer account.
* **Conversational Data:** Incoming and outgoing WhatsApp chat logs, automated AI replies, customer contact numbers, user-defined system prompts, and custom knowledge base documents.
* **Technical & Usage Analytics:** IP address, browser type, device details, session logs, dashboard interactions, and platform latency metrics.

### 2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
We use the collected information solely to provide, secure, and optimize AISA Connect™:
* To authenticate accounts, facilitate billing, and manage system access.
* To route, process, and automate conversational flows via official WhatsApp APIs.
* To train and refine your local, private AI models (your knowledge base data is kept secure and isolated).
* To generate real-time conversational analytics and reporting on your Client Dashboard.
* To comply with legal requirements and Meta's partner verification standards.

### 3. DATA SHARING & PRIVATE INFRASTRUCTURE
AISA Connect™ operates under strict data isolation guidelines:
* **No Selling of Data:** We **never** sell, rent, or monetize your business or customer data to third-party advertisers or data brokers.
* **Meta API Routing:** Your customer communications are securely routed through Meta's official servers as required by the WhatsApp Business API infrastructure.
* **Third-Party AI Models:** To process natural language, data may be securely transmitted to encrypted, enterprise-grade LLM endpoints (e.g., Google Vertex AI, AWS, or Azure). No customer data is stored or used by these providers for training foundational models.
* **Compliance & Law:** We may disclose data only if required by a court of law, regulatory authority, or to protect the safety and rights of UWO™, our Clients, and the public.

### 4. DATA SECURITY & ENCRYPTION
We employ advanced security protocols to keep your information secure:
* **Encryption in Transit:** All data sent between your systems, our servers, and WhatsApp APIs is fully encrypted using SSL/TLS 1.3.
* **Encryption at Rest:** Sensitive integration credentials, API tokens, and customer contact lists are secured using AES-256 standard database encryption.
* **Database Isolation:** Client databases are logically isolated to prevent cross-tenant data leaks or unauthorized cross-access.
* **Access Controls:** Strict internal access protocols restrict database access to verified security personnel only.

### 5. YOUR RIGHTS & CONTROL
Under global regulations (including GDPR, CCPA, and DPDP India), you possess the right to:
* **Access & Export:** Request a complete export of your conversational logs and contact databases.
* **Correction:** Edit or update your billing details and account credentials anytime from the Settings panel.
* **Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"):** Request the permanent deletion of your account, API integrations, and all associated conversational logs from our production systems.
* **Opt-Out:** Opt-out of non-essential marketing emails while continuing to receive critical billing and system alerts.
